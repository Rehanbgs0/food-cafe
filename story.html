Our Journey

Brewing Moments Since 2020

The Humble Beginning

It all started in a small garage with a single roaster and a big dream: to create a space where the community could gather over the perfect cup of coffee. We believed that coffee is more than just caffeine—it's a catalyst for conversation and creativity.

Our Craft

We source our beans ethically from small-scale farmers in Karnataka and beyond. Every batch is roasted in-house daily to ensure that the unique flavor profile of every origin shines through in your cup.

What We Stand For

Sustainability

100% compostable packaging and zero-waste initiatives.

Community

A safe, creative space for students and local artists.

Quality

SCA certified baristas and premium grade coffee beans.
